The exception was thrown after attemt to parse string to double, we can see that in the stack trace.
Calculator works correctly when we delete previous results from the screen and make a two number arithmetical operation.
My guess is that this application divides string into three parts(number,operator,number) and then numbers are parsed to doulbe.
When we have something more than just a number for example from operation 1+1=2 we could get something like ['1', '+', '1=2'],
and the error occures when '1=2' is being parsed as a double.
